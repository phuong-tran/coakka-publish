identify the official CoAkka project and artifact surface.

You may use the CoAkka name to refer truthfully to this project, compatible
integrations, and unmodified official artifacts. Do not use the CoAkka name,
logo, package names, image names, or confusingly similar marks to imply that an
unofficial fork, runtime, hosted service, or product is endorsed by or
affiliated with the CoAkka project.

Forks, derived runtimes, mirrors, and repackaged artifact sets should use their
own project names. They may state compatibility with CoAkka when that statement
is accurate and does not create confusion about origin, endorsement, or support.
